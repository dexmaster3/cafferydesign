Colleen Caffery Design

Professionally designing home interiors since 1996. Now designing in the Charleston area.

Classic French Interior Design

This 2006 home was designed for a client that wanted a timeless french look , one that would last for years to come. I used natural travertine and medium color quarter sawn oak floors. I cater to each individual client. I won't do anything theme like or too trendy, I tend for a classic yet current look. I am currently working in the old village of Mt pleasant on one 1980's house and one 1870's. Each job is unique and takes a different approach. I design everything from the floors, tile, bathrooms, kitchens, paint, wallpaper, all lighting , all furniture , rugs , antiques , paintings, prints, beds and bedding. it is a soup to nuts approach. I even design the landscaping and hardscapes. Working with many sub contractors over the years has served me well, I have many sources for all areas of design. I work on a hourly basis or more preferred , a job overview with an estimate and all design costs passed to the client with all original receipts and my percentage added. My phone number is 843 352 7646. Email Colleencaffery@aol.com. I am very flexible and cater to each client's taste, timeframe and budget.
